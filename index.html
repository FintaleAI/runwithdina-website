Your First Week

Everything you need before your first session

We want first-timers to feel clear, not intimidated. Here is what your first few days with RunWithDina usually look like.

Before You Join

  • Send a WhatsApp message with your current fitness level.
  • Share whether your goal is weight loss, stamina, or race prep.
  • Get guidance on which session is the right starting point.

What To Bring

  • Comfortable running shoes and easy workout clothes.
  • A water bottle for Vadodara heat and humidity.
  • A willingness to start at your own pace without comparison.

Who Usually Joins

  • Beginners who want fitness without a gym-heavy atmosphere.
  • Working professionals who need dependable 6:00 AM structure.
  • Runners preparing for 5K, 10K, half marathon, or longer goals.

FAQ

Questions Vadodara first-timers usually ask

These are the practical details people often want before they message or show up for the first session.

Can complete beginners join?

Yes. Many people start with low stamina or after a long break from fitness. Sessions are coached so you can scale effort and build up gradually.

Do I need to be preparing for a marathon?

No. Some members train for races, while others join for daily energy, weight management, mobility, and overall fitness.

What happens in Vadodara heat or monsoon?

Sessions stay practical and season-aware. Intensity, hydration, and movement choices are adjusted so you can train smartly through changing weather.

Where should I come for the first session?

Message Dina first. You will get the exact meeting point, day, and reporting time based on your fitness level and goals.

What should I carry?

Wear comfortable shoes, bring water, and arrive a little early. That is enough to begin.

How do I know which session type is right for me?

Share your current routine, any past injuries, and your goal on WhatsApp. Dina can suggest the best starting path before you attend.
